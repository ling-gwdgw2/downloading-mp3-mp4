# Privacy Policy (นโยบายความเป็นส่วนตัว)

Phoebe Downloader is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our desktop application handles user data.

## 1. No Data Collection (ไม่มีการเก็บข้อมูล)
Our software runs entirely locally on your computer (as a Local Web App on `localhost / 127.0.0.1`). 
- We **do not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
- We **do not** track your download history, URLs entered, or search queries.
- All downloaded files are saved directly to your local storage.

## 2. No Third-Party Sharing (ไม่มีการแบ่งปันข้อมูลแก่บุคคลภายนอก)
Since we do not collect any user data, no information is shared, sold, or distributed to any third parties.

## 3. External API / Network Requests (การเรียกขอข้อมูลเครือข่ายภายนอก)
The software connects directly to public APIs and video hosting services (such as YouTube and Spotify) solely to fetch media streams and metadata requested by the user. These requests are governed by the respective privacy policies of those external websites.
